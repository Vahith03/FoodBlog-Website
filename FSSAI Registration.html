Basic License

The Basic registration of food license is usually done for hawkers and businesses having sales of less than 12 lakhs. However in some cases the criteria is different. The government fees for Basic Registration is Rs. 100.

State License

The State license is usually done for medium sized businesses having sales of more than 12 lakhs but less than 20 crores. However in some cases the criteria is different. The government fees for State License can vary from 2000 to Rs. 5000.

Central License

The Central license is usually required for large businesses with annual sales of more than Rs. 20 crores. It is also mandatory for some businesses like import export to get a central license. The government fees for Central License is Rs. 7500.

things to know

Who needs a Food License?

Every business that deals in any kind of food products is required to have a food license.

What is the government fees ?

The Government fees can range from Rs. 100 to Rs. 7500 depending on your business. For more clarification you can call us at +91 9928380610.

Do I need multiple licenses for multiple products ?

No, One Premise having multiple kind of businesses is required to have one license only.

What if I have operations in more than 1 state ?

If a Food business Operates has Operations in more than one state, One additional central license for Head Office is required.

Once taken, For how long is the Food license valid ?

Food License can be applied for 1 to 5 years. After Expiry the license needs to be renewed.
